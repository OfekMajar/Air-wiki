What are Air Jordan's?

Air Jordans, often referred to simply as "Jordans," are a popular line of basketball shoes and athletic footwear associated with the iconic basketball player Michael Jordan. The Air Jordan brand is a subsidiary of Nike, Inc., and it was created in collaboration with Michael Jordan in 1984.
